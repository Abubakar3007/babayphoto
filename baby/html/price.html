Frequently asked questions and their answers!  

When should I book a newborn photo shoot?

Preferably during pregnancy. If you register after the birth, we cannot guarantee you an appointment, as we are often well booked months in advance.

When is the right time for a newborn shoot?

As the name suggests, the shoot should take place as soon as possible after the birth. The ideal would be within the first 10 days. Later I can no longer guarantee typical newborn photos. The babies are no longer so flexible and can no longer be brought into the special poses. In the case of fruit, the period is somewhat more generous.
It doesn't matter if your loved one has newborn acne or small scratches on their skin during our photo session. We can easily retouch these later in post-processing.

How much does a newborn photo shoot cost me? 

You can calculate everything exactly when you look at our price list. There are no additional costs.

Do I have to pay cash?

As you wish! You can also transfer the money. At the end of the shooting you will receive the invoice with the bank details. 
AFTER receipt of payment, the photos will be sent by us!

Do I get the files or printouts?

The photos are not printed. You can download your full-resolution photos from an online gallery and use these files freely.

Do I need to bring anything to the baby shoot?

No! Except what you would otherwise take with you when you leave the house. Pacifiers, diapers, bottles, ...

Where is the shooting taking place?

We have a studio in Seyring where we can offer you lots of accessories.
 

Do I need to bring accessories?

No!!! We have enough accessories! Both for baby belly shootings and for newborn shootings! For baby belly shootings you should pack underwear of the same color (1x white/1x black)!

What should we wear?

During our shootings it is very warm so that your baby feels completely comfortable and can sleep soundly! So wear something light so you don't sweat! For the family photos, I recommend simple colors and clothes without prints and logos! White, grey, beige, black,…
Best of all, you are coordinated!

How long does a newborn shoot take?

A newborn shoot usually lasts 1.5-3 hours.
We don't look at the clock, the end result, namely beautiful and relaxed pictures, will put all events in the background!

How long does it take to get the pictures?

After the shooting, it takes about 10 working days for the images to be processed. We will send you the link to the online gallery as soon as the money has been credited to our account. 

Are there also weekend appointments?

Basically, photos are taken on weekdays, but every now and then you can make an exception.
